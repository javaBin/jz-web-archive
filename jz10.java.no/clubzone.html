ClubZone

ClubZone is JavaZone's legendary party. With more than 2,000 attendees, there is an obvious need for meeting places outside the conference and exhibition spaces as well as in, a need that JavaZone accommodates with a variety of social events. Along with individual partners, JavaZone is sponsoring entertainment and refreshment at four different venues in Oslo city centre during the conference. We call this ClubZone and we think it will offer something for every taste.
