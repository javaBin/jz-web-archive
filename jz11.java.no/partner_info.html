Partner Info

As a JavaZone X partner, you have a unique opportunity to showcase your capabilities to developers, whether you are looking to build your brand, promote your products and services or create a forum for your developers. But participation in JavaZone X is more than that: it is also a place where you yourself and your staff have access to valuable new knowledge from the world’s most popular technology platforms. This reason alone makes JavaZone X worth attending.

As a partner and exhibitor you will have a prominent role at the conference. Activities are not confined to the auditoria. In the exhibition space, we have organized a range of activities, from whiteboards, where speakers can expand on their presentations and answer questions arising from the presentation, to music and stand-up performances. And an array of other activities will contribute to making the exhibition space itself a key meeting place for conference participants.

Being a partner makes you part of a vibrant, dynamic community, helps you develop your professional environment, and offers your staff a valuable professional and social boost. The intensive and comprehensive program offers your staff the opportunity to acquire useful skills and exchange experience with other software developers.

Partner package

JavaZone X offers a comprehensive partner package including:

  • Branding on the web, in the program, conference display material and advertising.
  • Four admission tickets.
  • Exhibition space, including a 6 m2 ready-to-use stand.
  • Access to discounted tickets.

A new feature this year is flexible partner tickets allowing access to the exhibition space only.

Branding

As a partner, you are automatically included in a variety of branding activities relating to JavaZone X. These include:

  • Promotion in press coverage before, during and after the conference. JavaZone has a strong focus on promoting the partners in all media contact and through distribution of press releases and other press-related material. JavaZone will also be cooperating closely with the technical press in connection with the conference.
  • Potential for individual exposure in the media: both the national daily press and technical press will be present and covering JavaZone.
  • Promotion in teaser activities
  • JavaZone X features a new profile in the program, auditoria, exhibition space and elsewhere.
