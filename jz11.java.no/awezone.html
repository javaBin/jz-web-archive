AweZone

ClubZone is cancelled! AweZone is born!

The last few JavaZones we’ve arranged a social gathering in three nearby pubs and clubs, with concerts, stand-up and networking over refreshments. This event was called ClubZone.

JavaZone goers have loved the acts, the mingling, and the party! However, this year we’ll be trying out an even bolder concept: We’re renting a much larger venue, with several subvenues inside. Thus, we’ll gather everyone at JavaZone X, under one roof, for the IT-party of the year!

The venue is Rockefeller, and includes the main stage area and its sub-stage, John Dee in the basement, and a cool roof terrace! Here we’re arranging concerts, stand-up and chill-out zones, and there will be refreshments as in the previous years!

We’re calling this new concept AweZone Festival - Don’t miss out!
