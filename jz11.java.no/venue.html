Oslo Spektrum

JavaZone X is held in Oslo Spektrum, in the heart of Oslo, surrounded by the Oslo City shopping mall and Hotel Oslo Plaza. Oslo Spektrum is within walking distance of the city’s main street, Karl Johans Gate, and the Oslo S/Airport Express train station. Oslo Spektrum is the venue for the country’s biggest concerts and it is here that major international stars who are visiting Oslo take the stage. The exhibition arena is exceptionally well suited to a conference like JavaZone X, with a variety of meeting places both within and outside the exhibition space itself.

Expo

The exhibition space at JavaZone X is a central meeting place for partners and attendees. All of the conference partners will be here. During the conference, this area becomes a real melting pot of activities, with events organized by the individual partners and centrally by JavaZone X. There will be a variety of competitions with great prizes to be won. There will be talks and discussions linked to the presentations, along with more social events.
