JourneyZone

JourneyZone is a post activity for all JavaZone X speakers. The Journey will explore the amazing Norwegian wild nature, great local food, activities and of course it will be possible to get to know the other explorers both on a personal- and a professional level.

After arranging several annual conferences, the JavaZone Committee observed that our speakers tend to travel around and see other parts of Norway on their own. In 2010 we arranged the first annual JourneyZone across Besseggen with accommodation at Memurubu. We had 30 explorers mixed with Vikings traveling both from Norway and abroad. The trip was a great success and even the Norwegians found the trip well worth to participate. Pictures available on Google Picasa

This years journey goes to Lyngen and visiting Fablab Lyngen, a MIT Research resort. From here we will enjoy walking in the mountains, local food and hopefully catching our own food fishing big fish form the sea. As technology enthusiasts we will also have some sessions on Friday and play around in the laboratory.

The Journey is open to all JavaZone X speakers, lightning talks included. In case of free seats JavaZone Crew members are allowed to join. A fee of just 1400 NOK will be charged. We have 35 seats. First come first serve.

Equipment

Bring:

  • Hiking Shooes
  • Rainproof clothing
  • Warm clothing (Average temperature outside in September is 5-10 degrees Celsius plus wind (don’t know what that is on the rubbish Fahrenheit scale)
  • Wool underwear
  • Wooly hat
  • Gloves
  • Small backpack to carry all above
  • Your camera and a lot of film. This will be epic!
  • Browse or translate www.turistforeningen.no s article for other things that could come handy

Do not bring:

  • Shoes with high heals and fancy dresses
  • Food and drinks

Practical information summarized:

  • Transport, accommodation, food, guides and all is arranged for you. You do not need to book any other than signing up to program@java.no
  • The activities can and will be adjusted to all skill levels, but some physical experience is an advantage
  • Bring clothing as described in the equipment paragraph.
  • Departure from Oslo Airport Gardemoen should not be booked before 8PM Sunday September 11th.

More detailed information will be provided closer to the conference. For questions and registration get in touch with us at program@java.no
