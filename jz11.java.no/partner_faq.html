Partner FAQ

How does my company become a JavaZone partner?

Buy a javaBin partnership and a Basic Pack for partners.

The javaBin partnership depends on the size of your company:

  • Small, up to 15 memberships – NOK 5000,-
  • Medium, up to 30 memberships – NOK 7500,-
  • Large, up to 50 memberships – NOK 10000,-
  • Extra large, up to 100 memberships – NOK 12500,-

The Basic Pack for partners is NOK 71,000, with an order deadline of 1 sept 2011. There is a special Early Bird rate of NOK 56,000 for orders made by 1 March 2011.

Please bear in mind:

  • “First come – first served” for the stand location (based on the date partner payment is received)!
  • There are price differentials for Norwegian/non-Norwegian javaBin partnerships!

(The above details are subject to minor amendments. All prices are ex. VAT.)

What does the Basic Pack for partners include?

The Basic Pack includes 4 tickets, profiling, stand location (with power, lighting, table, chairs, sign, carpet and floor space of 6 square metres) and access to more partner tickets for reduced prices.

Partner tickets

Being a JavaZone partner one can buy partner tickets. These come at the following prices:

  • 10-30 tickets: NOK 4150 per ticket
  • 31-50 tickets: NOK 4050 per ticket
  • 50+ tickets: NOK 3950 per ticket

The final deadline for buying partner tickets is 1. July 2011. After 1.July one will have to buy ordinary tickets.

Can the stand be extended?

Yes, stand options can be ordered from 1 April 2011, as follows:

  • Extra Stand Module (6 square metres): NOK 17,500
  • Extra Stand Module(15 square metres): NOK 26.000

(The above details are subject to minor amendments. All prices are ex. VAT.)

How do I order the Basic Pack for partners?

  • Please send your order to partner@java.no
  • An invoice for the Basic Pack and javaBin partnership will be issued to the partner
  • A partner contract will be sent to the partner for signing

How do I order the JavaZone X partner tickets?

  • The partner may order a minimum of 10 tickets (in addition to the 4 tickets in the Basic Pack for partners)
  • Please send your order to partner@java.no
  • An invoice will be issued to the partner
  • Tickets are issued to named individuals, but partners are free to change the names.

What if I need to issue a Purchase Order (PO)?

  • On request, javaBin will send you details by e-mail as the basis for your PO
  • The PO needs to be sent to partner@java.no as soon as possible
  • An invoice will then be issued to the partner

What about the speakers?

Speakers at JavaZone X will have free admission.

How can I get more information about my partnership?

Send an email to partner@java.no, and we are happy to help you, and to inform you about time and place for the next partner meeting.
