Press

During JavaZone, journalists and photographers will be able to submit copy and photos to their editors using an on-site open network.

Accreditation

Accreditation of journalists and photographers can be done in advance or at the start of the conference. JavaZone will invite journalists to a meeting where the organisers will give a briefing on the programme, the various offers and events and also provide relevant material such as news releases and photos.

If you require accreditation, please contact Andreas Røe, who is responsible for media relations before, during and after the conference.

If you would like more information about Javazone,
please contact Andreas Røe, ROE@no.ibm.com or tel. 974 07 125.

Press desks and refreshments

JavaZone's secretariat is equipped with press desks for journalists and photographers. Coffee, tea, snacks and mineral water will also be available. The secretariat has reserved a separate room for interviews.

Briefing

JavaZone will be inviting the media to a briefing at the opening of the conference. We will be providing details of seminars, exhibitors, events taking place during the conference as well as practical information. The secretariat will have press kits from exhibitors and the organiser, press information, conference programs, and photographs from the exhibition.
