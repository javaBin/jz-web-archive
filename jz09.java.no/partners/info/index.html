Partners

As a JavaZone partner, you have a unique opportunity to showcase your capabilities to developers, whether you are looking to build your brand, promote your products and services or create a forum for your developers. But participation in JavaZone is more than that: it is also a place where you yourself and your staff have access to valuable new knowledge from the world's most popular technology platforms. This reason alone makes JavaZone worth attending.

As a partner and exhibitor you will have a prominent role at the conference. Activities are not confined to the auditoria. In the exhibition space, we have organised a range of activities, from whiteboards, where speakers can expand on their presentations and answer questions arising from the presentation, to music and stand-up performances. And an array of other activities will contribute to making the exhibition space itself a key meeting place for conference participants.

Being a partner makes you part of a vibrant, dynamic community, helps you develop your professional environment, and offers your staff a valuable professional and social boost. The intensive and comprehensive programme offers your staff the opportunity to acquire useful skills and exchange experience with other software developers.

Registration for JavaZone partners opened in mid-February. Within a few days, 30 partners had joined up, and there has been a constant influx since then, but we still have a few places left. Act quickly to secure one of the remaining places.

Partner package

JavaZone offers a comprehensive partner package including:

  • Branding on the web, in the programme, conference display material and advertising.
  • Four admission tickets.
  • Exhibition space, including a 6 m2 ready-to-use stand.
  • Access to discounted tickets.

    • A new feature this year is non-personalised partner tickets allowing access to the exhibition space only.

    Branding

    As a partner, you are automatically included in a variety of branding activities relating to JavaZone. These include:

    • Promotion in press coverage before, during and after the conference. JavaZone has a strong focus on promoting the partners in all media contact and through distribution of press releases and other press-related material. JavaZone will also be cooperating closely with the technical press in connection with the conference.
    • Potential for individual exposure in the media: both the national daily press and technical press will be present and covering JavaZone.
    • In teaser activities
    • This year JavaZone features a new profile in the programme, auditoria, exhibition space and elsewhere.
