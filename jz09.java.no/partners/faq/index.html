Partner FAQ

How does my company become a JavaZone partner?

Buy a java.no partnership and a Basic Pack for partners.

The java.no partnership is NOK 5,000 for companies with java developers working in Norway and NOK 25,000 for companies without java developers in Norway.

The Basic Pack for partners is NOK 60,000, with an order deadline of 1 sept 2009. There is a special Early Bird rate of NOK 47,000 for orders made by 1 April 2009.

Please bear in mind:

  • "First come – first served" for the stand location (based on the date partner payment is received)!!!
  • There are price differentials for Norwegian/non-Norwegian java.no partnerships!

(The above details are subject to minor amendments. All prices are ex. VAT.)

What does the Basic Pack for partners include?

The Basic Pack includes 4 tickets, profiling, stand location (with power, lighting, table, chairs, sign, carpet and floor space of 6 square metres) and access to more partner tickets.

Partner tickets

Being a JavaZone partner one can buy partner tickets. These come at the following prices:

  • 10-30 tickets: NOK 3950 per ticket
  • 31-50 tickets: NOK 3850 per ticket
  • 50+ ickets: NOK 3750 per ticket

The final deadline for buying partner tickets is 1.June 2009. After 1.June one will have to buy ordinary tickets.

What does a java.no partnership include?

  • Profiling on java.no
  • Discounts regarding different kind of partnerships (for instance JavaZone)
  • Advertisement at java.no

Being a java.no partner one gets the opportunity to become a partner at javaBin`s extraordinary seminars and arrangements where JavaZone is the most well known conference.

Can the stand be extended?

Yes, stand options can be ordered from 1 April 2009, as follows:

  • Extra Stand Module (6 square metres): NOK 15,000
  • Meeting Module (6 square metres): NOK 22,500
  • Lockable room (as an integral part of your stand): NOK 2,500

(The above details are subject to minor amendments. All prices are ex. VAT.)

How do I order the Basic Pack for partners?

  • Please send your order to oez@mesan.no
  • An invoice for the Basic Pack and java.no partnership will be issued to the partner
  • A partner contract will be sent to the partner for signing

How do I order the JavaZone partner tickets?

  • The partner may order a minimum of 10 tickets (in addition to the 4 tickets in the Basic Pack for partners)
  • Please send your order to oez@mesan.no
  • An invoice will be issued to the partner
  • Tickets will not be printed or despatched until full payment is made
  • Tickets are issued to named individuals, but partners are free to change the names up until 1Â September 2009. After that, each change will be subject to a fee of NOK 750.

What if I need to issue a Purchase Order (PO)?

  • On request, javaBin will send you details by e-mail as the basis for your PO
  • The PO needs to be sent to oez@mesan.no as soon as possible
  • An invoice will then be issued to the partner
