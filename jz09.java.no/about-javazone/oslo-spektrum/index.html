Oslo Spektrum

The venue

JavaZone is held in Oslo Spektrum, in the heart of Oslo, surrounded by the Oslo City shopping mall and Hotel Oslo Plaza. Oslo Spektrum is within walking distance of the city's main street, Karl Johans Gate, and the Oslo S/Airport Express train station. Oslo Spektrum is the venue for the country's biggest concerts and it is here that major international stars who are visiting Oslo take the stage. The exhibition arena is exceptionally well suited to a conference like JavaZone, with a variety of meeting places both within and outside the exhibition space itself.
