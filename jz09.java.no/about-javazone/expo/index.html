Expo

The exhibition space at JavaZone is a central meeting place for partners and attendees. All of the conference partners will be here. During the conference, this area becomes a real melting pot of activities, with events organised by the individual partners and centrally by JavaZone. There will be a variety of competitions with great prizes to be won. There will be talks and discussions linked to the presentations, along with more social events.

Relax/gamezone

The exhibition space is also where we give in to our natural human frailties. There are limits to what the body can tolerate, over the course of two days, in our quest for knowledge. So at JavaZone, between the action, we let you abandon yourself to old vices! Relax/gamezone is our take on the watering hole, a chance to reenergise yourself playing computer games. Here you will find hammocks and beanbags, Nintendos, Playstations, X-boxes and worse besides!
